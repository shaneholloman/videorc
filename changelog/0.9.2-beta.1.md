If Videorc 0.9.1 showed you a black camera or a silent microphone even though
permissions looked right in System Settings, this release is the fix.

## The capture fix

The installed app was missing a piece of its macOS security configuration, so
the system quietly denied camera and microphone access no matter what you
granted. 0.9.2 ships correctly configured, and our release checks now verify
this on every future build before it can go out.

**If your camera still looks blocked after updating:** toggle Camera off and
on for Videorc once in System Settings → Privacy & Security (the old build may
have left a stale denial behind).

## Delivered by the updater

This is the first release that reaches you through **Settings → About &
updates** — no re-download needed. If you're on 0.9.0 or 0.9.1, check for
updates and you'll be offered 0.9.2.

## Also improved

- The native preview is more dependable when launching the app and when
  switching sources — fewer blank frames, faster recovery.
- Recording with image/asset backgrounds is more robust.
