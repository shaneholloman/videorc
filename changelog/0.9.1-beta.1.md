Videorc 0.9.1 is about never having to download a DMG again.

## In-app updates

Open **Settings → About & updates** and Videorc checks for a new version,
downloads it with progress, and installs it on restart. If you're recording or
streaming, installing waits until you're done — an update will never cut a
session short.

This release also surfaces the app version in Settings, so it's always clear
what you're running.

## Known issue — fixed in 0.9.2

In this build, **camera and microphone capture didn't work in the installed
app**, even with permissions granted in System Settings (screen recording was
unaffected). 0.9.2 fixes it — and thanks to this release, the fix arrives
through the in-app updater.

## Everything from 0.9.0

Local screen, camera, and microphone recording, the native detached preview,
and the signed, notarized macOS build — all still here.
