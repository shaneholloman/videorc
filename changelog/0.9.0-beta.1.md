This is the first Videorc beta — the foundation of the local recording studio.

## What you can do

- **Record locally.** Capture your screen, camera, and microphone straight to
  disk. Pick sources, arrange your layout, and record — no streaming account
  required.
- **Set up with a real preview.** The detached native preview window shows
  exactly what the recording will capture, so you can check framing and layout
  before going live to disk.
- **Install with confidence.** The app ships as a signed and notarized macOS
  DMG with everything bundled — no separate downloads or command-line setup.

## Requirements and notes

- macOS 13 Ventura or later, Apple Silicon first.
- Downloads are available to signed-in Videorc accounts.
- macOS will ask for camera, microphone, and screen-recording permissions on
  first launch — that's expected.
- Auto-updates and Windows builds are not part of this first beta.
