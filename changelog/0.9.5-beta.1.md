## Captions stay with their video

Recording several captioned videos in a row could burn the previous video's
last caption into the start of the next one. Fixed at the root: caption
display state now resets for every session, late transcripts from a previous
video are rejected everywhere, and a new session can never inherit a caption
bar.

## Docked preview, refined

- Switching tabs (including ⌘1–⌘9) now takes the docked preview off screen
  completely — nothing lingers over other pages — and it returns the moment
  you're back on Studio.
- The "Open preview" placeholder is now exactly the size of the preview
  itself, so the Studio layout doesn't jump when you open, stick, or close it.

## Backgrounds and assets

Imported background images showed a "Missing" badge and refused to display,
even though the file was saved correctly. They now load reliably, and
"Missing" only ever appears when a file is genuinely gone from disk.

## A calmer, clearer Studio

- The yellow setup warnings are gone from the top of the Studio; if something
  blocks recording or going live, the reason now sits right next to the
  disabled button, with a link to the page that fixes it.
- The audio mixer shows a live level while you record or stream — you can see
  your mic working at a glance. When idle, "Check level" still gives you an
  instant reading.
- With captions on, the leftover instructional sentence is gone.

## Scene page, simplified

- The confusing "Scene sources" list is gone — the stage diagram is the source
  list. Click a source on the stage to edit it; visibility lives in the
  inspector.
- Selecting a full-screen source no longer shows a panel of dead arrows — it
  plainly says the position is fixed for that layout.

## Library and updates

- Recording problems in the Library are now a compact issue badge — hover to
  see the details instead of scrolling past a wall of warnings.
- When an update is available, a notice appears at the bottom of the sidebar;
  one click restarts into the new version when it's ready.

## Multistreaming is Premium

Streaming to more than one destination at once now properly unlocks with a
Premium subscription — signed-in Premium accounts get it automatically, and
it stays locked otherwise, with a clear upgrade path on each additional
destination.
