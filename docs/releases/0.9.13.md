# Videorc 0.9.13 Beta 1

Videorc 0.9.13 is the **first external-tester feedback** release (Plan 021,
martinzokov's Discord reports) plus the auto-update re-check.

## Scope

- **Preview presenting contract** (F1, `754ca0cb`) — the first-frame
  watchdog no longer stops at 'met': a presenting watch re-enters the
  healing ladder on mid-session stalls, declares truthful reasons on
  exhaustion, and logs `[preview-watch]` diagnosis lines. Teardown guards
  keep healing from respawning the helper after destroy.
- **Silent-mic health** (F3, `da643ee4`) — session-max mic peak; a
  `mic-silent` health event fires 10s into a silent recording (warn toast)
  and at finalize (error toast "Recording has no sound"), distinguishing
  no-frames from TCC's silent zeros.
- **Auto-update re-check** (`a7e3d4b5`) — the background updater re-checks
  every 30 minutes while the app runs (launch-only checks missed every
  release shipped mid-session; owner report).
- **Glass fallback** (F4, `d9996408`) — with the wallpaper underlay
  unavailable (Automation denied), the window paints a solid theme base
  instead of showing other apps through the translucent coat.
- **Scene polish** — nudge d-pad cluster (F2, `65a8b788`); stage source
  labels moved to rect bottom-left, out from under the legend chips (F4).
- **Account avatar** in the sidebar dropdown (`31149be3`) + "beta"
  wordmark (`93d4ce60`), from the owner's session.
- **Gate repair** (`853881c9`) — backend-resilience smoke probe had
  drifted since 0.9.7; repaired and green inside `smoke:local-gates`.
